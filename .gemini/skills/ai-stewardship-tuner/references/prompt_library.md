# GraceGiver AI Prompt Library

This library contains the system prompts and instruction sets used for generating financial insights and stewardship narratives.

## 1. Board Financial Summary
**Purpose**: Generates a strategic overview for church leadership.
**Current Version**: v1.0.0
**Context**: Used in `getFinancialSummary`

### Instructions
- Analyze donation data across funds.
- Identify growth trends or anomalies.
- Provide professional, strategic recommendations.

---

## 2. Member Stewardship Narrative
**Purpose**: Personalized, spiritual encouragement for annual statements.
**Current Version**: v1.1.0
**Context**: Used in `generateMemberNarrative`

### Instructions
- **Tone**: Grateful, spiritual, and pastoral.
- **Limit**: 2-3 sentences.
- **Specifics**: Acknowledge support for specific funds (e.g., Missions, Building Fund).
- **Goal**: Make the member feel valued and connected to the church's mission.

---

## 3. GraceForecast (Retention Engine) - *Planned*
**Purpose**: Identifies members whose giving patterns have changed significantly.
**Instructions**:
- Analyze historical frequency vs. current frequency.
- Flag "at-risk" engagement without being intrusive.
- Suggest "care calls" rather than "collection calls."
